Find out how Marlon Zarate, your GEICO Insurance Agent in San Mateo, could help you save money on more than just your car insurance.

Hello, my name is Marlon Zarate. I have been an insurance agent for over 10 years, and I opened my local GEICO office in San Mateo in 2015.

From San Francisco to Mountain View, my office would be happy to help Peninsula area residents see how much they could save on more than just car insurance. Ask us about motorcycle insurance, boat insurance, ATV insurance, RV insurance, and more. We can also help with homeowners, renters, condo, or mobile home insurance.

My office will help you with any GEICO discounts you may qualify for, such as discounts for Military members or members of certain alumni or professional organizations.

For our Spanish-speaking customers, we have full-time Spanish-speaking agents in the office. Representantes que hablan español están disponibles en esta oficina.

Our goal is to offer you the best possible coverage, at the most competitive price, with excellent customer service! Call today for a quote.

Spot Marlon and his team around the San Mateo community.

I enjoy giving back to the community by participating in local events and charities. I am an active member of the City of San Mateo Chamber of Commerce. I also participate in many of the Downtown San Mateo Association events, such as the Downtown Clean Up. Keep an eye out on our Facebook page for upcoming events and fun giveaways. You may even meet the Gecko!

Stop by the San Mateo GEICO office.

We are conveniently located on El Camino Real between the 92 Freeway and Downtown San Mateo. We'd love to meet you!
